Meet Our Founder & MD/CEO

Surv. Amb. M.M Kabir

mni, fnis
Founder & MD/CEO
Global Leadership in Geospatial Science

Chairperson, FIG Africa Regional Network

At Emkay Surveys, our commitment to precision is inspired by the global standards and vision of Surv. Amb. M.M Kabir. As a leading figure in the international geospatial community, his work ensures that the surveying profession in Africa remains competitive, innovative, and aligned with world-class practices.

Driving the African Geospatial Agenda

Following a distinguished career in regulatory leadership, Surv. Amb. Kabir serves as Chairperson of the International Federation of Surveyors (FIG) Africa Regional Network. In this capacity, he leads efforts to modernise land governance and professional standards across the continent, bridging the gap between local expertise and global innovation.

A Vision for Private Sector Excellence
  • Registrar / CEO, Surveyors Council of Nigeria (SURCON) — The national body for the registration, regulation and control of the survey profession in Nigeria.
  • State Surveyor-General, Katsina State — Administered a jurisdiction of 24,000 km² with a population of 10 million people.
  • Chairman, Forum of all States Surveyors General in Nigeria (FOSSGON) — Coordinating survey policy across all 36 states.
  • Member, Presidential Technical Committee on Land Reform (Land Use Act) — Contributing expertise to Nigeria's national land policy at the highest level.
  • Member, FIG Strategy Group — Representing Africa and large associations globally within the International Federation of Surveyors.
  • Nigerian Delegate, UNGGIM-Africa — Actively representing Nigeria's interests in the UN Committee of Experts on Global Geospatial Information Management for Africa.
  • Panel of Experts — UNGGIM-Africa Action Plan — Representing West Africa in the review of the continental geospatial action plan.
  • Panelist, 1st UN World Geospatial Information Congress — China, 2018; one of the foremost global gatherings on geospatial knowledge.
  • Nigerian Delegate, UN 4th High Level Forum on Good Land Governance — Advancing responsible land administration at international level.
  • Panelist, World Bank Global Land Tools Network Conference — Washington DC; shaping global frameworks for land tenure and tools.
  • Regular Contributor, UNGGIM-Europe — Bridging African perspectives into European geospatial governance discussions.
Professional Distinctions
  • DFID GEMS 3 Award for Promoting SLTR in Katsina State — Recognised by the UK Department for International Development for excellence in Systematic Land Title Registration.
  • Women-in-Surveying Angel of the Year (2016 – present) — Sustained recognition for championing gender inclusion and equal opportunity in the surveying profession.
  • National Good Governance Award 2020 — Conferred by a coalition of 86 Civil Society Organisations in recognition of exemplary public service.
  • Pan-African Leadership Prize for Excellence (ASUP 2020) — Honouring continental impact and leadership in the geospatial sector.
  • Thomas Sakara Leadership Prize for Integrity and Transparency (YOUPAD) — Awarded by youth-led professionals for ethical leadership.
  • Fellow of the Nigerian Institution of Surveyors (fnis) — Awarded for lifelong contributions and technical mastery.
  • Member of the National Institute (mni) — A graduate of NIPSS, Kuru, bringing high-level strategic policy expertise to the private sector.
  • Global Ambassador — Recognised as an "Icon of Nation Building" and a key partner in implementing the African Union's agenda on land.
  • 📊 33 years of professional experience, with 28 years at top management level.
  • 📄 25+ published local and international professional papers in surveying and geospatial science.
  • 🤝 Registered 2,000+ members into the Young Surveyors Network, growing the next generation of professionals.
  • 🌍 Arranged the FIG President's State Visit to Nigeria — meetings with the President of the Federal Republic and the President of the Senate.
