Who We Are

Built on Trust & Accuracy

Emkay Surveys Nigeria Limited is a full-service surveying and engineering firm headquartered in Abuja, with a nationwide presence spanning the North-West and North-Central regions of Nigeria.

Our multidisciplinary team of licensed surveyors, engineers, and technology specialists work with precision and integrity — ensuring every project is completed on time, within budget, and to the highest professional standards.
